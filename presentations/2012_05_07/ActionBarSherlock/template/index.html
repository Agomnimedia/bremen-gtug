ActionBarSherlock

ActionBarSherlock 4.0

  • Compat-Lib + ActionBar = ActionBarSherlock
  • porting full 4.0 ActionBar-API to 2.1 and higher
  • "quasi"-standard in the Android devcommunity
  • recommended by Googlers
  • developed by +Jake Wharton
  • Android enthusiast, working at Square Inc., SF

ABS: get it to work



  • import the library project
  • extend com.actionbarsherlock.app.SherlockActivity or com.actionbarsherlock.app.SherlockFragmentActivity
  • further Sherlock-classes...
    • com.actionbarsherlock.app.ActionBar
    • com.actionbarsherlock.view.Menu
    • com.actionbarsherlock.app.SherlockFragment
      • ...

ABS

ABS: get it to work

  • adapt theme in AndroidManifest.xml: 

    <!-- Theme festlegen -->
<application android:theme="@style/Theme.Sherlock"... >
...
   <!-- ActionBar für eine bestimmte Activity nicht verwenden -->
   <activity android:theme="@android:style/Theme.NoTitleBar"... >

  • further Sherlock-themes... 

    <application android:theme="@style/Theme.Sherlock.Light"... >
...
<application android:theme=
	"@style/Theme.Sherlock.Light.DarkActionBar"...>
